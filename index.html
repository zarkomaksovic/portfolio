Getting started

Introduction with project.

This is the first step of making a perfect web page for you. At this stage I'm going to find out what is it that you wish to achieve with your web page, are you going to use it to improve your bussines, promote something or someone etc. Do you have any specific requirements in the way of looks, static and dynamic side of your page...

  • Relax
  • It doesn't take that long.
  • But it is only the beginning.

Research

Your desires are most important.

This page is built for you. What you want is what counts. Now I'm going to find everything I can about specifics of your field of interest. I have to get to know you or your job so I can do mine flawlessly.

  • Easy
  • Simple Q and A
  • No wrong answers.

Design

Finding best solution for you.

You are free to go, for now... Now it's my turn to do the hard work. At this step, I'm finding the best look, general style, interactivity, animations, and all the other stuff you see on internet that will make your page attractive to others, but not too tacky. My goal is to make them stay with you as long as possible.

  • Don't worry
  • Marker is't permanent.
  • I clean my screen after every project.

Coding

Making your dreams come true.

OK. This is it. Now the fun starts for me. I'm turning my design into code that any browser on any device will be able to read and turn into web page. The importance of this step is that code must be written correctly and dynamically so you can change things if and when you want, without giving yourself or me a headache.

  • Coding is easy
  • Making client smile isn't.

Testing

Making sure that your page works with no mistakes.

Now I'm going to spend almost the same hours testing your page, as it took me to make it. This is also hard work and equaly important. We dont want to see errors after we make our page go live. Mistakes happen, but it is my job to find them and treat them in time. One last but very important thing. You are now my main tester. We are not going on the web without your approval.

  • Test...test... one-two-three..
  • I think it's good to go!
  • Do you agree?

Going live

Your page is going on web, and everyone can see it.

D-day. Feeling excited? Me to, and it happens every time. You are now live on web. Your friends, customers, bussines associates can easily reach to informations they need. You can turn onto other parts of your life and bussines. It's going to be easier now, you'll see.

  • So long...?
  • Not so fast.
